Company

The best in quality and professionalism delivered on schedule and on budget

Techone LTD. based in Samsun/Turkey has been providing our clients superior design, installation and services. We pride ourself on working with our clients to provide a Project that fits their budget and timeline every time. Our staff is the backbone of our success, from our Project Manager, Designer and Estimators, to our Project Superintendents, Masters, Journeyman and Apprentice Electricians.

TECHONE Electrical Contractor and its staff have the expertise to ensure your projects are done properly by trained professionals. TECHONE Electrical Contractors serves the Industrial, Agricultural, Municipal and Commercial markets. We have our roots in motor control and process electrical systems, but we have grown and expanded, allowing us to service customers and projects of all sizes. We specialize in USA Based Project which requires NFPA- 70(NEC).

At TECHONE Electrical Contractors, “Safety is First”, we are very proud of our safety record. Continuous training for our entire staff ensures that our electricians will perform safely and exceed the requirements your project demands per NFPA-70E. All keyperson of TECHONE has been trained by OSHA certified trainer for 30 hours at least.

TECHONE also has `Master Installer and Designer` for Lightning Protection and Grounding System certified by Lightining Protection Institute in USA. TECHONE Electrical Contractors, Inc., specializes in OBO Projects requires NFPA-70 complying with Industrial, Agricultural, Municipal and Commercial projects such as: American Embasy Project (Design input, procurement support, shop drawings, material take off, material submittal process, installation, tests & commissioning… etc)
